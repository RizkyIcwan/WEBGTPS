Welcome  

SANSTOPIA

SANSTOPIA Private Server is a safe and enjoyable space for players, with strong moderation and a zero-tolerance policy for disruptive behavior. It’s known for being welcoming and inclusive, allowing players to be creative without fear.

Join a universe of limitless worlds, all interconnected. Build incredible worlds with friends and fill them with anything your heart desires! Collecting seeds, growing trees, and combining seeds to create new ones are at the core of the game. SANSTOPIA is filled with everyday heroes — and a few villains too!
